### About Me

You can call me Junjie.

Everyone named Junjie seems to get called `JJ` by the people around them.

`JJ` can be romanized as `zheizhei`, and with double-pinyin that becomes `vwvw`, which is where `vw2x` comes from.

I have been using double-pinyin for two or three years. The short, fixed rhythm gets me into flow within seconds, so I recommend it. (Though in the big vibe-coding era, maybe speaking to the computer instead of typing will work even better 😳)

I like solving different kinds of problems. I am currently in Nanjing doing advanced mobile security work, mainly focused on device-level adversarial research.

Code and more projects are on [Github](https://github.com/vwww-droid).
